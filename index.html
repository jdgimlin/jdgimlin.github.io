About Me

Hello, I’m John and I’m seeking a new career in data analysis. I’ve been studying and working in data analytics and data science for over two years. My specialties and best skill sets lie in data procurement, cleaning and filtering, descriptive analysis, and visualization.

Though I’ve worked a lot with Python and R, I’m most adept with SQL, spreadsheets, and visualization software like Tableau.
